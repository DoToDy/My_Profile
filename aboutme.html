My name is Dominic Dys.

I enjoyed watching animations, looking at art listening to music, and was always in awe at how can someone make these things a reality, i then realised that this is also something i would want to do, to create something that others could enjoyed. And so, i found myself on this path, improving at my own pace, hopefully making someone be in awe and inspired the same way i was.

Some of my hobbies include drawing and collecting various things, I also sometimes draw concepts for a Roblox game called Doodle World.
